Job Overview

We are looking for a Senior Product Designer to join our core product team. You will be responsible for leading the design lifecycle of our flagship HR SaaS platform, from initial discovery and user research to high-fidelity prototyping and design system contribution. You will collaborate closely with product managers, engineers, and stakeholders to create seamless, intuitive experiences that empower recruitment professionals worldwide.
